Who are We?

Welcome to our Hussain Leathers, established in 2003 and based in Ambur, a small town in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. We started with nothing but a passion for creating high-quality leather products, and today, we are a well-established brand in the industry. Our journey began with a small team of skilled craftsmen who had a shared vision of creating timeless and durable leather goods. We started by producing handmade leather shoes and accessories, which quickly gained popularity among the local community. Our commitment to quality and attention to detail helped us build a loyal customer base, and our reputation soon spread beyond Ambur. Over the years, we have expanded our product range to include leather bags, wallets, belts, and jackets. We source only the finest quality leather from ethical and sustainable suppliers, and our skilled artisans use traditional techniques to craft each piece by hand.



Thank you for taking the time to learn about us. We look forward to serving you and sharing our love for leather with you.
